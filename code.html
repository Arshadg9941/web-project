Institute of Management Kolhapur College

Your gateway to leadership and professional success. Join us to take your management career to the next level.

The Institute of Management, Kolhapur, which is a constituent unit of Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed to be University, Pune, was set up in 1994 under the mentorship of Dr. Patangrao Kadam. The objective of setting up the institute was to provide quality education in management sciences.

Courses

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

It provides a comprehensive understanding of software development, programming languages, and related concepts. MCA programs are designed for students seeking to specialize in areas like data management, IT infrastructure, and system design. .

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA degree is a 2-year postgraduate qualification designed to equip students with advanced business skills and knowledge. It typically covers a wide range of subjects including finance, marketing, human resources, operations, and strategy. One can get MBA degree online as well as offline..

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate degree program focused on business management and administration. It prepares students for careers in various business sectors by providing a solid foundation in core areas like marketing, finance, and human resources. Students can also choose to specialize in areas such as international business or digital marketing. .

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

The BCA curriculum typically covers fundamentals of computer science, programming languages, data management, networking, web designing, and operating systems. Graduates can pursue careers in software development, web development, data science, and other IT-related fields.

About Us

Till 2005, the Institute was affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur and was brought under BVDU, Pune as a constituent unit from the academic year 2005-06. The institute has made a mark for itself in the field of management and in addition to being accorded recognition by the Government of Maharashtra, it has also been approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The MBA and MCA programmes offered by the institute have also been given approval by the AICTE. The Institute has a huge building covering an area of 32,258 sq. ft. with classrooms measuring as much as 710 sq. ft. There is a spacious library spread out over 2600 sq. ft. with 16,300 books and 114 journals. It is a matter of pride that the papers of our highly qualified faculty members are published in both national as well as international journals.
